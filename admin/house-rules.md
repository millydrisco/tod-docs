# House Rules

## Prepared Casters
All spellcaster classes for the purposes of any campaign that I run, are prepared casters.
What this means in practice:
* All casters (except Subclass Casters, i.e. Arcane Trickster and Eldritch Knight) can change levelled spells during a long rest
* No casters can change their cantrips once selected
* We still have the difference of known vs full list access (specified in the table below) except it’s slightly different to ‘canon’

| Class | Full List Access (FLA) or Known Only (KO) | Rational |
|---|---|---|
| Artificer | FLA | This is canon |
| Bard | FLA | Full Caster: Bards have dedicated their lives to magic through performance, the weave flows through them in art and they’re not necessarily aware of how they’re casting but it comes as naturally to them now as breathing. |
| Bloodhunter (Order of the Profane Soul only) | KO | Not a full caster - you have other shit up your sleeve |
| Cleric | FLA | Full Caster: God provides innit. | 
| Druid* | FLA | Full Caster: You gain your magic through the earth around you, as long as it answers your call, you can cast. |
| Paladin | FLA | Arguably I would have made you KO but this is a canon event and you have like 3 spells. |
| Ranger | FLA | Arguably I would have made you KO but this is a canon event and you have like 3 spells. |
| Sorcerer | FLA | Full Caster: The magic is literally in your blood bruv, like why would you be limited. | 
| Warlock | FLA | Full Caster: You have a magical sugar daddy, you’ve just gotta ask nice and look pretty. |
| Wizard* | KO | Please see below |

_*Wizard_
_So it may seem odd that Wizards aren’t joining the ranks of full casters in having full access to their spell list. But this is because I think Wizards only having access to a wizard spell list is stupid. If there are spell scrolls for all class spells then a Wizard should be able to learn it due to their years of bloody study._

_So wizards are known only because you start off with being able to know and prepare wizard spells, but I have no issue with a wizard learning spells from any class because you literally went to school for this._

_It just makes no sense to me that someone who has an in-depth knowledge of how the weave works wouldn’t be able to figure out Healing Word for example. Silly._

## Extra Spells and Attacks
There are times in combat or even in roleplay that casting a spell or doing an extra attack makes thematic sense. Maybe your mother is in peril, maybe you’ve just been dealt a fatal blow but want to protect your friends with your final breath because thats what your character would do. 

I’m here to make a great fucking story with my friends, so if you’re convincing and have conviction you can roll a d10:

| Roll | For Casters* | For Martial Classes* |
|---|---|---|
| 1-3 | You take a level of exhaustion equal to your spell casting modifier (no higher than 5) | You take a level of exhaustion equal to your STR or DEX modifier, whichever is higher (no higher than 5) |
| 4-6 | You take a level of exhaustion equal to half of your spell casting modifier (minimum 1) | You take a level of exhaustion equal to half of your STR or DEX modifier, whichever is higher (minimum 1) |
| 7-9 | You take 1 level of exhaustion | You take 1 level of exhaustion |
| 10 | You cast the spell with no ill effect | You take the action with no ill effect |

## Death Saves
Death saves are to be rolled privately, preferably by real dice and you send me the picture of it. I will then describe the visions and memories that your player is experiencing. 
Then you guys will need to work out things are going from interpreting the dreams/experiences. I feel like this is a good balance between metagaming death saves and being completely in the dark. 

## Combat Timing
In order to keep combat snappy and feeling tense, we’re going to implement a timer (not too strict) of 3 minutes per turn.

You can modify this by saying one of the following:

| Phrase | Effect |
|---|---|
| _Down One!_ | This will move you down one turn in the initiative order for that round |
| _HELP!_ | This will add 5 minutes for others to help with your turn |
| _Fuck off!_ | This will skip your combat turn for that round| 

Obviously this is flexible, we’re not being super strict but we’re going to try and keep to the irrational decisions that your characters would make in a combat situation.
