# Absences and Missing Sessions!

I've been pretty strict with absences from the game for the last 5 years, but it's time for a change. It makes me feel like I'm tracking absence like work or school, which isn't fun for me or you as players. Equally, I don't want people feeling guilty for making it so we miss out on game time and I'm starting to really lose track of where we are in game not going to lie.

## What does absence mean?
You're going to miss one or more session for whatever reason, I don't mind what the reason is. No one is going to be mad at you and if you feel like someone is getting pissy at you about it - let me know and we can have a chat about it. We're all adults. 

## Why have I been so strict with it in the past?
I try to make sure everyone has their own story arcs, whether they're big or small, publicly known about or private and just for you. There are hints and bits of the story that are sometimes written just for a particular character, there are sometimes climactic bits that need all of you to be there because I've balanced things so all your skills are needed to get through. Sometimes, I just really need you all to bully Mattie. 

I've just wanted to provide the best experience whilst making sure nobody misses out - but I can see now that people are missing out in different ways so hopefully the new "policy" is a better way around? Feedback is always welcome!
