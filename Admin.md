# Admin

## Absences/Missing Sessions!

I've been pretty strict with absences from the game for the last 5 years, but it's time for a change. It makes me feel like I'm tracking absence like work or school, which isn't fun for me or you as players. Equally, I don't want people feeling guilty for making it so we miss out on game time and I'm starting to really lose track of where we are in game not going to lie..

So...trialling as of June 2026, the following rules will apply:

### Expected Absence
If you know you're going to be away for a session and you're one of the [Core Group](https://github.com/millydrisco/tod-docs/blob/main/README.md#core-group), you should have access to the google calendar - update it. This links to updating the discord bot that posts every Monday. 

**Take the following steps:**
1. Try to have a conversation in discord to rearrange the session so we can still all play. *It is up to you to then change the session date in the Google Cal, please, so it reflects the correct day.*
2. If a new day can't be arranged - let Mattie know! It's on them to sort Vecna shit out, none of my business.

#### Caveats
* An expected absence is one with like 24hrs notice or more. We can all pretty much sort stuff out with 24hrs notice.
* If the expected absence is due to something emotional, or otherwise impacting that would mean the above steps are too much - reach out, I don't mind doing the work.
* These steps also apply to me.

### Unexpected Absence - The shit has hit the fan
You didn't see this coming, you're sick, your car broke down, you stabbed a guy and now you have to hide a whole body! Whoopsies, sometimes these things happen!

No one is going to throw a fit. This is the policy that is changing. 

I need you to reach out to *me specifically* or at least tag me in your post in the discord. If we're in the middle of a story arc that is directly related to **your character** it's going to be tricky to continue without you, but I *should* by now have gotten from all of you your preferences on how to handle your missingness for 1 or 2 sessions. 

I'm going to need you to do a little bit of work for some of them though so bear that in mind.

## Options for Absence Handling

|Status| How I Will Integrate Into Session | How You Will Return to Next Session | My Ask of You, the Player |
|---|---|---|---|
| On Leave| "Off Screen Actions" - This is best suited to expected absences, we can talk about what your player will be doing and how this might impact your party and their plans. Yes, Mattie, we can do this for your army of dormant PCs if you want? | Solo quest completion and reporting to the party, you can send messages in the discord in character in the Campfire Chats channel at anytime and let them know how your quest is going throughout your absence. | Think about what kind of things would pull your character away from the campaign, what would be important enough to them and what would a resolution look like. I'm a good storytelling but my brain is dogshit and full of demons a lot so help me out! |
| Active Support | "Limited NPC Control" - Best for last minute drop outs where it wouldn't make sense for you to disappear but you don't want me to fully play your character. I'm not going to let anyone else take the reigns, but I have got a feel for when most of you would help and when you wouldn't at this point. You can fully give me a flowchart to help me decide if you don't trust me. | You'll return as normal to next session, a summary of the session will be posted in the Campaign Notes channel (if I forget prod me) | Think about why your character might have been quieter that session. I don't want to ask too much here in case someone uses this whilst they are sick! |
| Background Role| "Silent Observer" - You don't want me to fucking touch your precious baby and that is a-ok. They became a piece of the decor, in a barrel, popped into the teapot and never came out the entire time. If you are integral to the story arc at this point, we will need to cancel the session. Or do something Non-Arc Related because your character got the sniffles. | Similar to Active Support, you will just slot back into the session as if you never left, though experiencing strange flashbacks of the previous session throughout like you had temporary amnesia of the events and are having to recall things you witnessed like an outer body experience. | Think about reasons why you might have been quiet. And why you don't trust me. What do you think I'm going to do to your character Matt? Sam? What do you think I'm gonna do, huh? |

For longer term absences, I will come up with actual quests for your characters (and you) to complete in a bit more of an interactive way. (Looking at you, Angel, with your upcoming time zone struggles) I want people to have a bit more agency in how their characters are involved and how they are absent in the story. It is still collaborative storytelling at the end of the day!!

The most important thing in this is the communication around what your character is thinking and doing while they are away, where we are in the story and how it impacts the story. We can be quite unpredictable in how much we get done in a session and I suck at admin! Session notes are not my forté and I don't want you guys missing key information that impacts your ability to get your characters storylines completed or moving!
