# Session Log Summer '26

## 23/06/2026
After following the group who were convinced that dining with Yuan-ti was a grand plan (Vark, Jenny, and Wyll), Frank discovered that they were in fact under the suggestion spell. The group were led to a prison in an underground temple, where they met Aisha and Clawdia - the last of their group to have survived being kept as sacrifices to the Pool of Divination kept in the Tomb of Diderius. 

Aisha and Clawdia, it turns out had been sent by the Alliance council to back the team up in their fight against Chuth and the Green Wyrmspeaker - however they had been waylaid on their journey. They were asked to verify some intelligence gathered by the council, that Varram the White Wyrmspeaker had been spotted in Serpent Hills, as it was only briefly out of their way to supporting the adventurers. Unfortunately, the Yuan-ti capturered them along with Varram and his group in the area around Diderius' Tomb. 

The party managed to go back and forth on the sending stones (The Vodastone Network - credit Stuart) and a daring escape was conducted. A giant snake (so inconspicuous), a tiefling bard hurriedly trying to paint herself as an egg, and several completely undisguised people I guess just walking along singing "don't be suspicious" directly in front of all the people in the temple somehow passed all the stealth and deception checks to get to the prison. They bust out everyone and then confronted the head honchos holding Varram, because why not.

They essentially pulled a Misha/Bugs Bunny style "I'm your boss, this is a random inspection", and ended up walking out (with Varram in tow) after deciding the Pool of Divination looked icky. I didn't say this in session but the battle with Chuth ended in the late afternoon early evening, meaning as you came out of the temple it has started to get fully dark - you are late into the night now. The GM writing these session notes then stared at the ceiling for a while...
