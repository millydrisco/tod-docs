# The Feywild Arc - 6 Ches to 26 Tarsakh 1489 DR

## Aurora’s Quest – Side Quest 1
-	Entered the Feywild to meet Aurora, Lady of the Summer Court worried about the state of balance over the two courts – the party hoped that they could bargain with the fey to restore the Weave but alas if their magic was also unbalanced that seemed unlikely that they could help. 
-	Aurora asked them to help solve the murder of the Winter Lady – a restriction had been placed on the mantle to mean it could only pass onto someone willing by a powerful wizard, which had never been seen before, and they were running out of time. 
    * The summer court suspected that the missing villagers were changelings that had been placed there to be watched over by their parents at a close enough distance to watch them grow up but far enough to be safe from fey court drama. A changeling could take on the mantle and become a fully-fledged Archfey 
    * Their spies had seen emissaries of the Wild Hunt dealing with strange fellows in purple robes (Cult of the Dragon) – the party went to investigate in exchange for help with the Weave situation (upon completion).

### Heading to the Hunt
-	They headed off to find the villagers and found them in a clearing where the party in fact killed most of them trying to help them escape. They encountered the Master of the Hunt, who told them he appreciated their bloodlust and would give them an opportunity to escape his clutches if they dealt with his son, Halen – bringing him back into the fold to take on the mantle of the Huntmaster. They accepted
-	They were accosted by a Crocodilian Lizardfolk called Gustave II on the revenge path for Elenya’s crusade of reptilian conversion, after terse words were exchanged; in order to escape the clutches of the Master of the Hunt he agreed to work with the party until they escaped the Huntmaster.

## Master of the Hunt’s Quest – Side Quest 2
-	The party had to enter a Labyrinth, where they faced a number of challenges including:
    * Navigating a Sylvan forest with awakened trees and Dryads (where Elenya obviously set fires) 
    * Escaping an oubliette filled with Ettercaps
    * A dream ball where each party member was faced with a phantom from their past and the party learned something about each other
        - Anguish confronted her ex-wife whose sister she accidentally killed
        - Elenya saw her ex-lover and the parent of her children, Cass, and the response broke the heart of Cassyt with whom a new romance had been blossoming
        - Creswinda saw her parents, and actively avoided confronting them the entire time
        - Mardred played happily with the wolves that raised her
        - Hirsch saw the old Goliath that he lived with before the incident ™ 
        - Gus saw a vision of Sobek, his God, and almost broke down with the fear of failing him
        - No one seemed to notice that no one appeared for Cassyt
    * After this, their hosts appeared Halen and his partner Damh, Archfey of the Domain of Music, Dance and Joy, (pronounced d-ah-v) who explained that Halen had run away several times to be with his lover and that his father couldn’t accept that he didn’t have the cruelty in him to be Master of the Hunt. 
-	The party agreed to refuse the Master of the Hunt, they went to the agreed meeting on the way there through a hall of mirrors. Some fucker touched one of the mirrors and they all had to fight mirror versions of themselves (hell as a DM – I would not recommend, truly) 
    * They decided to give the MotH the Mirror Halen and again, miraculously passed charisma checks. Real Halen feels indebted to the party and would support them, they have not mentioned this debt to anyone though, I think they’ve probably forgotten?
    * They learned here that Cassyt was a Changeling not a half-elf.
-	Damh says he will reward the party with a break somewhere fun, they try to protest but he is the Archfey of Joy, and he will do as he pleases…

## Witchlight Carnival – Side Quest 3
-	They arrive at the entrance of a carnival, if they were frequent multiplanar travellers they would have known to be excited, but they weren’t. They peruse for a while enjoying various treats and amusements, some highlights:
    * Mardred wildshapes into a giant snail while at the Pixie Kingdom, she commits Pixie Genocide as the tonic they made her drink to be pixie sized does not affect snails.
        - She did win the snail race though so props to her
    * They saved the relationship of the sad, mute clown and the mermaid
    * They discovered the sinister taking over of the carnival from Zybilna, the Archfey of the Domain of Prismeer, by the two Shadar’kai, Mister Witch and Mister Light
        - They learn the secret of crossing over into Prismeer from a spy in the carnival who stole it from the Shadar’kai. They were warned about the Hourglass Coven – a trio of Hags that have splintered Prismeer into three domains for them each to govern, Hither, Thither and Yon.
        - They are asked to go into Prismeer and save Zybilna from Temporal Stasis so she can defeat the Hags 

## Prismeer
### Hither
-	They go to Hither and make their way through the Soggy Court (Bullywugs), and to the first Hag’s cottage. 
    * On their way to the Soggy Court, they meet a scarecrow named Clapperjaw. 
    * The first hag is Bavlorna Blightshaw, she does taxidermy and the team steal a bunch of stuff including a unicorn horn, a loom, some cursed thread and some mushroom hooch. 
    * They preach unionisation to her workers; the kitchen staff (a redcap called Bloody Toes) seems particularly on board and reveals that Bavlorna is allergic to anyone who runs widdershins, before they fall to their death.
    * They murder the hag by boiling her in her favourited piss pot and hooch, and then find Iris hiding in a cupboard having been captured at some point by the Hag. Iris joins the Party. 
-	Clapperjaw guides them to Thither

### Thither
-	First thing in Thither they go through to a cave in the middle of a forest, a blind old man (Nib) gave the party gifts but seemed to be testing them to see if they were safe. 
    * They realised that Clapperjaw was made of child meat and so Iris killed him with Dispel Magic. The party developed The List™
-	They were guided through the back of the cave and met the Getaway Gang
    * These were children that escaped from the Hag here, including Lamorna and Will of the Feywild who will help the party deal with the Hag. There are wanted posters everywhere for Will who seems like a small boy but otherwise has the skills of a high level adventurer. 
    * They also meet Squirt the Oil Can who is made of flesh and made continuous innuendo, everyone hates Squirt. Squirt was my revenge. Squirt needs them to collect slippery boggle oil for him which they do. 
-	They met Lamorna the Unicorn who was looking for her mate (Elidon; they found her mate in the Hag’s house and freed him by placing his horn on the rocking horse in Skabatha’s house) she was attacked by Zarak and Iris cast Fast Friends on him to convince him to give up on Unicorn hunting. 
-	The characters spend some time in the Goblin Market and then raid Skabatha Nightshade’s workshop saving the children and killing both Skabatha and her Jabberwock (the green dragon she has as a companion). 
-	Elenya licks books. They’re mimics. They figure out the family tree – Zybilna is the adopted sister of the Hags, and they have placed her in stasis with the use of her own cauldron. 
-	They free the children with the help of Will (who they discover is in fact an Oni taken human form to protect the children). Except Lamorna, Lamorna stays with Anguish as she can’t remember whether she has parents or not.
-	Squirt transports them to the heart of Prismeer by squirting on them and making gross noises. They skip Yon altogether. 
Heart of Prismeer – the Palace of Heart’s Desire
-	They are placed to wait with other petitioners, many of which have been waiting centuries and may actually die if the temporal stasis is lifted. 
-	The party go through many trials and find a few interesting things including chromatic roses and eventually battled the remaining foes looking to keep Zybilna locked away (as well as some who were protecting her and couldn’t be convinced that they were trying to help). 
    * Zybilna granted them one wish spell but none of the party can remember what it was for or any of Zybilna’s other names…except for Iris. 

## Back to Side Quest 1? 

Zybilna transported them back to the clearing where they met Aurora, who seemed in a better mood. She informed them that while they had been away, the wizard Harry Dresden had solved who had done the murder – so they didn’t need to worry, however the matter of who would take the mantle was still a problem. 

-	Here is where Elenya tried to cast a fucking spell and got hit by a meteor and insta-died. 
    * She had a conversation with her God, Pelor, and was given the option to come back as a Divine Soul Sorcerer which she accepted.
    * At the same time – and crucially this deal closing simultaneously so we will never know what power brought Elenya back to life - being stricken with grief, Cassyt offered herself to take the Mantle of the Winter Lady in exchange for Aurora casting Revivify as no one in the party had it prepared. 
    * Elenya ultimately chose to go down the path of Pelor and given the powers of the Sun God and the Winter Court are incompatible she could not be a Warlock of Cassyt’s.
    * Anguish chose to take Cassyt’s Patronage rather than lose her forever to the Feywild. 

Aurora reminded them that according to intel from the purple hooded ones operations were continuing on the Sword Coast and so Cassyt teleported them there as her parting gift.
At some point between this point and when I next mention it Cassyt gifts people tattoos of Cold resistance and a magical teapot dimension so they will be safe from everything including her.
