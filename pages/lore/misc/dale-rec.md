# Dale Reckoning - A Primer on Time

The main campaign takes place in the year 1489 Dale Reckoning (DR). Dale Reckoning is only one of several calendars used in Aber-Toril, but it is the most widely known across Faerûn. 

It began when the Standing Stone was raised by the Elves of Cormanthyr and the Humans of the Dalelands, marking the beginning of the period where humans have been permitted to settle in more open regions of forest. Sometimes called Freeman's Reckoning.
