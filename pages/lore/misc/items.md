# Notable Story Items, Weapons and Magic Artefacts

## The Phlan Skulls

| The Deceased and their Bearer | Background | Effect of the Item |
|---|---|---|
| Anguish carries Sepurcia de Gadea | She was the wife of Phlan's most prominent Mob Boss who mysteriously disappeared after her husband was found dead. Rumour is she found out about his child labour practices and killed him herself, not even the Kelemvorites know how her skull got into the catacombs. | WIP |
| Creswinda carries Niklas Bohm | He was a lawyer who managed to somehow die of old age while being the town gossip - knowing everything about everyone. He was a very wealthy gentleman and generally well liked - seemingly harmless, though the people who disliked him tended to end up behind bars... | WIP |
| Elenya carries Florinda | She was the daughter of a human nobleman who died at the age of 25 falling from her window while being serenaded by the blacksmiths half elven son. He was banned from visiting her in the catacombs and her afterlife consciousness has developed a terrible fear of heights. | WIP |
| Hirsch carries Zathran Alanarel | He was a doctor and community healer, generally not a chatty fellow he saved thousands of lives across his life, but regrets that he never got to see the world. He did fall in love though, he fell deeply in love with Ellisandra the lower town baker witch, he would call on her constantly and loved her dedication to her cat Mog. | WIP|
| Mardred carries the stowaway Mog | Unknowingly, Mog the famous Phlan skeleton cat snuck into Mardred's pack for a warm place to rest her bones. Ellisandra loved her cat so much that when Mog passed, she cast a necromancy spell so powerful that it outlived her by centuries - when Ellisandra was interred into the necropolis Mog followed and has been catching mice and bugs that find their way down into the depths.| WIP |
