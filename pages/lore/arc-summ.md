# Campaign Summary Notes

There have been three major arcs so far through the campaign that can be split into smaller arcs (of course), with each player having smaller personal journeys within. 

The main campaign arcs are:

- [The Phlan Arc](pages/lore/full-arcs/phlan.md)
- The Feywild Arc
- The Sword Coast Arc

The Campaign uses Dale Reckoning as it's in world calendar (an explainer to be added later - keep an eye out). I've done my best to give a general summary of the arcs below along with the in world dates they cover:

## The Plan Arc: 26 Alturiak to 5 Ches 1489 DR

The original adventuring group (Anguish, Creswinda, Elenya, Hirsch, and Mardred) met and laid the groundwork of realising something was going on with the Cult of the Dragon in Faerûn. They met Cassyt de Raera, a young half-elven Kelemvorite Cleric, and thoroughly corrupted her - stealing her from her home and bringing her along for the ride. Ultimately though, they were a force for good - getting rid of the undead in Phlan...until they then broke the very fabric of magic as it was known on the material plane and ran off into the Feywild to try and bargain to get it fixed.

Oh and Elenya started the reptilian crusades for Pelor - this wouldn't _usually_ deserve it's own standalone sentence, but it becomes surprisingly relevant.

## The Feywild Arc: 6 Ches to 26 Tarsakh 1489 DR

The gang met the Summer Lady and completely ignored her request for aid, killed a bunch of kids while trying to rescue them, set fire to a bush in a dryad protected forest, and rescued the runaway twink son of the Master of the Hunt so he could live with his boyfriend (who pointed out Cassyt was not a half-elf, but in fact a Changeling). This with the aid of the Crocodilian Lizardfolk hellbent on seeking revenge on Elenya for her swathe of reptilian conversion (a.k.a., Gustave II). All before continuing to ignore the Summer Lady and her request for aid in finding the killer of the Winter Lady and finding a replacement for her, and going to the Witchlight carnival where Mardred committed Pixie Genocide, Anguish became a renowned foecal painter, the group met Iris (with whom Anguish immediately adopted a child), and the group ultimately saved the realm of Prismeer. 

They then seemingly remembered the Summer Lady (quite an important person by all accounts), however Elenya cast a spell forgetting that they had broken the fabric of magic and was hit by a Meteor dying instantly. Cassyt exchanged her life as a Changeling to adopt the Mantle of the Winter Lady in exchange for Elenya's life, but Elenya had already made a deal with her Pelor and chose her God instead of Cassyt. Anguish chose to take up a Warlock Pact with Cassyt to keep her as part of the group however tangetially, Cassyt helped the group travel to the Sword Coast to catch up with the Cult (remember them?).

## The Sword Coast Arc: 27 Tarsakh to ‘Present’ DR 

This arc saw the group lose two members: Mardred to the threat of an old enemy to the pack that raised her, and Hirsch to finding a sweet baby Goliath that he felt should be raised amongst their own kind. Though gained two new members, Norvin - a stabby little shit from the Zhentarim and Vark - an upstanding member of the Waterdeep Guard. 

The group were landed in Waterdeep and found clues to the Cult's plans, joining a caravan heading North towards Castle Naerytar - on the journey is where they lost Hirsch - though whilst in Waterdeep they met one of Elenya' estranged daughters Edith. At the Castle, once again, Elenya caused issues for herself by getting pregnant and after giving birth and making a stupid deal with the now Archfey Cassyt they left via portal to a Hunting Lodge in Graypeak Mountains. Here they met with Grand Prince Tyomkin Grigory Alesnarovich (Grisha) of Iskal (See Korios Mini Campaign Notes [WIP]) who seemed to upset Iris but not too much, then Iris and Anguish adopted yet another child on the way to Skyreach Castle. 

Skyreach Castle involved a huge bust up fight with Elenya's new rapidly aged Bullywug x Tiefling twins and her old caretaker (Granny Gnora), which is when Mardred left. After which Vark and Norvin joined the group, the owner of the Castle joined their forces as an ally and they travelled to Waterdeep once more to become members of an elite council made to combat the Cult of Dragons. 

So far they have:
 - Rescued Maccath the Crimson from the Sea of Moving Ice, killing Araunthator of Lone Fang Mountain and his mate Arvaeiaturace (both adult white dragons)
 - Dealt with Grisha's misguided attempted to free Creswinda from her parent's attempts to marry her off by faking her death and freeing her mother from the Devil Graz'zt's control (he now lives in Iris' head and Creswinda has to regularly prune his penis)
- Rescued the Cassalanter Children from Asmodeus and gained the support of their family (Asmodeus now hitches a ride in Iris's head too)
- Killed both Neronvain (exiled prince of the Misty Forest) and Chuth (Adult Green Dragon) and may have gained the support of the current regent of the Misty Forest, Crown Prince Alagarthas
- Been invited to the Council of Dragons, though being able to attend is in danger due to everyone currently having wandered off to potentially be eaten by Yuan-ti.
- Learned that there may be a way to free Cassyt from her Mantle without killing her.
